# Chapter V — Guilds

One of the most active community structures within Blade Zenon was the guild system. Before the game was even released, players had organized themselves into competing factions with their own leadership hierarchies, recruitment processes, and rivalries.

Ryujinni introduced a formal rule on **August 7, 2015**: members could only belong to **one guild at a time**, citing community balance as the reason.

---

## Ðivided

**Founded by:** Aʞasa and Sȶar  
**Type:** Roleplay, Filming, PvP  
**Rival to:** The Royals

Ðivided was described as the *"Official Royals Rival."* It focused on three activities: roleplaying, filming, and PvP combat. Leadership was structured with formal ranks:

- **Leaders:** Sȶar, Aʞasa
- **Commanders**
- **Rookies**
- **Recruits**

Initial confirmed members included Sȶar, Aʞasa, and BigeCZ. The guild emphasized measured conflict. When the Chaos guild proposed endless wars, Ðivided pushed back — preferring occasional treaties over permanent hostility. The Chaos guild accepted this and both parties agreed constant war would be burdensome.

---

## Echoes of the Lost Age

**Founded by:** Darky  
**Type:** Roleplay

A roleplay guild welcoming up to 15 members with numbered slots. Darky posted an application form asking prospective members for their:

- In-game name
- Birthday
- Skills
- Reason for joining

Early members included **Andrew Sunshadow** and **Strawberri**. A previous thread for this guild had been deleted because, as Darky explained, "Bun-ny trashed the forums."

---

## Chaos

**Founded by:** Fain  
**Type:** Combat (Warrior / Battlemage / Melee / Assassin)

Chaos was openly combat-focused, positioning itself as conflict-driven from the start — a Warrior, Battlemage, Melee, and Assassin guild by design. The guild promised to outlast rivals. Its application process asked prospective members about:

- Online frequency
- Guild devotion
- Loyalty
- Qualifications
- Desired class and rank
- Nickname

**Leadership:**
- **Leader:** Spider
- **Co-Leader:** Fighter6968

Fighter6968 was among the first to sign up.

## The Royals

Referenced as the rival guild to Ðivided. No dedicated thread was recoverable, but its existence is documented through Ðivided's founding post labeling itself the "Official Royals Rival."
