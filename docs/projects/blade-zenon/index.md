## Overview

Blade Zenon was a fantasy game project and accompanying online community created by Ryujinni (then 12 years old) in 2015. The project was developed under the banner of **Studio Lygon X** and hosted on the ProBoards forum platform at `bladezenon.boards.net`.

The forum served as the central hub for the game's development, community organization, lore documentation, and player guilds. It attracted **181 registered members**, peaked at **61 concurrent users**, and accumulated **1,148 posts** across **155 threads** before going dormant in 2017.
