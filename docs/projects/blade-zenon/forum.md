# Chapter IV — The Forum

The Blade Zenon forum was structured into several categories, each serving a distinct purpose within the community.

### Public Boards
General activity.

| Board | Threads | Posts |
|---|---|---|
| Roleplay | 0 | 0 |
| General Discussion | 11 | 104 |
| Off Topic | 58 | 496 |

The **Off Topic** board was by far the most active section of the forum — 58 threads and 496 posts — reflecting a community that had come together around the *idea* of the game but spent most of its time simply existing together.

### Ideas
Design contribution boards.

| Board | Threads | Posts |
|---|---|---|
| Theories & Suggestions | 1 | 9 |
| World Design / Map | 16 | 68 |
| Discussion / Suggestions | 36 | 219 |

The suggestions boards combined held **53 threads and 296 posts**, making them the second-most active category after Off Topic. Players were genuinely invested in shaping the game.
