# Chapter VI — Decline

## The Breaking Point: August 2015

By August 2015, the community had begun visibly fracturing — before the game had released. The **General Discussion** board filled with threads about member departures and growing doubt.

Darky's thread **"My opinion about the game coming out"** (17 replies, 1,047 views) captured the mood. Members expressed concern about the release timeline, staff dynamics, and the general direction of the project.

BigeCZ posted **"My opinnion about ower staff team"** (14 replies, 678 views), airing grievances about the moderation and leadership. Lady Nanami's **"Truth About The Release Date"** attempted to manage expectations.

Threads titled **"people leaving."**, **"Moving on..."**, and **"I'm Sorry"** appeared in quick succession through August and September 2015, documenting a community that was dissolving faster than the game could be built.

## FreeRealms Sunrise

The proximate cause of the community collapse was the emergence of **FreeRealms Sunrise** — an unofficial revival project for the original FreeRealms game that drew away much of the audience Blade Zenon had recruited. 

Hiruko Kagetane later reflected on this in the shoutbox:

> *"People gravitated towards a false promise of a returning FreeRealms."*

From Blade Zenon's perspective, the community it had built to fill the FreeRealms void was simply reclaimed by FreeRealms itself — or at least by the hope of it.

## The Revival Attempt: November 2015

In November 2015, **Darky** posted **"Lets revive this"** as an announcement thread. The post acknowledged that members had migrated to the competing forum and declared: *"Blade Zenon will be revived and will be loved again."*

**Sȶar** pushed back, noting that Darky had also left previously. Darky's defense was pragmatic: Blade Zenon could serve as a backup if FreeRealms Sunrise failed.

**Hiruko** had been checking the forum daily. **Fighter** expressed interest. **♥ Starlight ♥** returned with renewed enthusiasm in November.

The revival did not hold.

## Silence: 2016

**Cosinus** posted **"Sooo... What happened?"** on December 24, 2016 — Christmas Eve — to no response. 748 views, 0 replies.

## The Last Post: May 2017

The final recorded activity on the forum was **OfficialByraine** posting in the "Lets revive this" thread on **May 20, 2017**, asking what Blade Zenon was. 

No answer is recorded.

## The Afterward

Hiruko Kagetane remained — the last documented presence, checking in every few months with no expectation of finding anything new:

> *"I'm arguably the last one who remains, but I only check every couple months in some sort of hope that something could have happened."*

The forum remains online, frozen in 2017.
