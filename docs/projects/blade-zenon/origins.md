# Chapter I — Origins

## The FreeRealms Context

Blade Zenon was born directly out of the shutdown of **FreeRealms**, a Sony Online Entertainment MMORPG that had been a significant game for a generation of young players. When FreeRealms shut down, its community fragmented, searching for something to fill the void.

Ryujinni — then 12 years old — decided to build that replacement himself. The forum records this clearly in a shoutbox message from community member Hiruko Kagetane:

> *"It was originally going to be a game to replace FreeRealms after it had shutdown, but things slowly fell apart."*

## Studio Lygon X

The project was developed under the banner of **Studio Lygon X**, credited in the forum's footer as the production entity behind Blade Zenon (2024 archival note).

## Release Date & Platforms

The game was officially targeted for release on **October 24, 2015**, with support announced for:

- **Windows**
- **UNIX Systems**

The forum carried an age rating of **13+**.

## The Forum Launch

The ProBoards forum at `bladezenon.boards.net` served as the project's public face — a place to post news, gather community feedback, coordinate guilds, document lore, and recruit players before the game's release.

Moderators were appointed early: **BigeCZ**, **Darky**, **Lady Nanami**, **Sȶar**, **Aʞasa**, and **Ryujinni** himself.
